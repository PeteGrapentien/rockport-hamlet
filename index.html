About the Campaign

Welcome to Rockport Hamlet — a homebrew Dungeons & Dragons 5e campaign set in the quiet hamlet of Rockport. This zine documents the sessions, stories, and lore as they unfold at the table.

Meteors have begun falling near the quiet village of Rockport Hamlet, each one arriving with violent lightning storms and stranger happenings in their wake. From the first crater came a mysterious crown now worn by a man called Vic, who has quickly become the village’s unlikely “Petty Baron.”

As more meteors crash nearby and reality itself begins to twist, a band of travelers must uncover the truth behind the storms, the crown, and the growing rifts in the sky. Somewhere beyond the haunted Gallowshade Forest lies the latest crater—and perhaps the key to sealing the fractures between worlds before something far worse comes through.

New sessions are published after each game night. Everything here is written from the perspective of in-world historians and unreliable narrators, so take the facts with a grain of salt — just like the characters do.

The Party

The adventurers at the center of it all.

❧

Mike

Human Bard · Level 2

A bard is historically a professional poet, storyteller, and musician—rooted in Celtic tradition—who composes and recites verses celebrating heroes, history, and genealogy. Often considered lorekeepers, they traditionally held high status for their ability to influence reputation through song.

☽

Christa

Human Rogue · Level 2

A rogue is defined as a dishonest, unprincipled person, scoundrel, or a mischievous individual who acts independently, often defying authority or established norms. They are characterized by stealth, cunning, and unpredictable behavior, frequently operating on the fringes of society or acting in a dangerous, uncontrolled manner.

⚔

Leah

Human Fighter · Level 2

A fighter is a person or entity trained in combat, characterized by high physical prowess, skill with weapons, and resilience, often engaging in battle or serving as a warrior, soldier, or expert in martial arts.

✦

Kevin

Human Wizard · Level 2

A wizard is a practitioner of magic who gains power through intense study, intellect, and research rather than innate ability. Typically depicted in fantasy as wise, robed figures with staves, they are versatile spellcasters who manipulate reality, control elements, and master arcane secrets, often specializing in specific schools of magic.

☩

Lex

Human Cleric · Level 2

A cleric is a fantasy RPG character class and a religious professional acting as an intermediary between the divine and earthly worlds. As heavily armored spellcasters, they utilize Wisdom-based magic to heal allies, damage foes, and combat undead, with powers derived from a specific deity or divine Domain.
